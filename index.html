Cannoli Fund

Medal Shop

Help us continue to give cats and dogs in need a better life and grab our cat and dog 2020 Fiesta
medals for only $5 each. Each medal comes with a bonus “Spay and Neuter” enamel pin! The
Cannoli Fund is an all-volunteer nonprofit organization.

All proceeds support our free spay/neuter and wellness programs for stray and owned King
William and Lavaca pets. We have provided free spay/neuter and important veterinary services to
more than 1,720 cats and dogs since 2011.
